How to make: Ham and Swiss Omelet

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup cubed fully cooked ham
  • 1/4 cup shredded Swiss cheese

Steps needs to follow

  1. In a small nonstick skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat.
  2. Whisk the eggs, salt, pepper, and water.
  3. Add egg mixture to skillet. (Mixture should setimmediately at edges.)
  4. As eggs set, push cooked eggs towards the center, letting uncooked portion flow underneath.
  5. When the eggs are set, place ham on one side and sprinkle with cheese; fold other side over filling.
  6. Slide omelet onto plate.
